Welcome to the amazing space adventure game! In this game you have crash landed onto Uranus after exploring the Andromeda galaxy. Your goal is to get the hyperdrive to work by collecting the following items: a doohickey, screw driver, and power crystal. Explore the nearby planets, make friends with the locals, and rush home before you die of old age. When you get back to Earth you will earn the title "Supreme traveler." Good luck!
